### 2\. What the Eurosky PDS and Portal are

**The Eurosky PDS** is infrastructure for hosting an [AT Protocol](https://atproto.com/) identity and storing and serving data associated with that identity. It enables use of compatible applications and services built on the AT Protocol. The Eurosky PDS is not a social media platform. It does not provide feeds, recommend content, rank posts, or curate speech.

**The Eurosky Portal** is a user-facing front-end available at [portal.eurosky.tech](https://portal.eurosky.tech). The Portal is currently available only to users of the Eurosky PDS; this restriction may be relaxed in future versions. The Portal allows Eurosky PDS users to:

- create a Eurosky account on the Eurosky PDS;
- sign in to their account using AT Protocol OAuth;
- view a dashboard with their profile information as fetched from their PDS;
- browse and view data stored in their PDS, such as images, videos, and other media;
- discover compatible third-party AT Protocol applications via a featured-apps directory;
- use sign-in facilitation for selected third-party apps, where the Portal shares your handle with the app to streamline its sign-in flow; and
- access account management and support links.

The Portal is currently in **beta**. This means it is an early version that is not feature-complete, and its features and behaviour may change.

The Portal is not an online platform: it does not host user content, operate feeds, recommend content, or curate speech.

The Eurosky migration tool [**EU-HAUL**](https://move.eurosky.tech/) is a separate product with its own Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. These Terms do not apply to EU-HAUL.

### 8\. The Portal — features and third-party apps

**Featured-apps directory.** The Portal includes a directory of third-party AT Protocol applications. Clicking a plain featured app opens the third-party application's website in a new browser tab. The Portal does not transmit your personal data to these applications.

**Sign-in facilitation.** For selected third-party apps, the Portal may facilitate the sign-in process by sharing your handle with the destination app so that it can identify you and streamline its own sign-in flow. Before any data is shared, the Portal will clearly inform you of what will be transmitted. Only your handle (and, where applicable, a marker identifying Eurosky as the referring service) is shared. The Portal does not share your OAuth access token, your email address, your password, or any other profile or account data with third-party apps through this feature.

**Data browsing.** The Portal may allow you to browse and view data stored in your PDS, such as images, videos, and other media. This data is retrieved from your PDS using your existing authentication and displayed to you within the Portal for your own use. The Portal does not independently store copies of this data and does not share it with any third party.

**Third-party responsibility.** Once you leave the Portal and visit a third-party application — whether via the featured-apps directory, sign-in facilitation, or any other link — that application is operated by a third party and is governed by its own terms and privacy policy. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or conduct of third-party applications.
